The Air Jordan 1 High “Pine Green” is the high-top version of Michael Jordan’s first signature model in yet another clean color scheme. Using the team uniform colors of the Boston Celtics as inspiration, the “Pine Green” makeup features a premium black leather base on the mid-panel, forefoot and collar. Green leather on the Swoosh, perforated toe, ankle collar, and heel contrasts the look. A blacked-out Wings logo can be found on the collar with contrasting red Nike Air detailing on the tongue tag. The colorway is finished off with a classic white rubber midsole and Pine Green outsole. Release date: February 29, 2020.
